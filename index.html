Leveling & Systems Guide

Start on Hard difficulty. XP bonus is worth it with Helltide's 50% XP. Move up when elites die fast.
Complete the LoH campaign first. Unlocks War Plans, campaign skip for alts, Mercenaries, Horadric Cube, Talisman.
Save Obols until 70. Vendors give 850 item power at max level. Huge head start.
Horadric Cube. Upgrade common → legendary. Best for target-farming uniques (e.g., gloves → Gohr's or Dawnfire).
Talisman system. Equip Seal + 5 matching Charms (set bonus) + 1 Unique Charm. Endgame sets are build-defining.
Mercenaries. Hire 1 + 1 reinforcement. Raheir (tank/DR) and Varyana (attack speed) most common. Aldkin (Field of Languish) for DR option.
Runewords. Ritual + Invocation in weapons/armor. Key combos: Moni+Ceh (Vulnerable), Cem+Gar (Crit), Igni+Prid (auto Dark Prison), Cir+Ceh (Freeze+Vulnerable).
Level cap 70. Pit Tier 10 → Torment 1. 14 extra skill points from Renown/Season Ranks (83 total). Paragon opens at 70.

Barbarian

Arsenal System · Shouts · Fury · Berserking · Dust Devils

Class Mechanic: Arsenal

Assign weapons per skill. Technique slot = passive bonus from any weapon type. WW → Two-Handed Slashing (Furious bleed). Polearm Technique for damage at high HP.

Whirlwind — hold one button, fire conversion via Bursting (lvl 15), Dust Devils spawn automatically, Gohr's auto-fires Mighty Throw. HotA/Ancient Leap — fire melee summoner, Ancestral modifier spawns Ancients mimicking swings, generatorless (spam HotA only), Bul-Kathos' Pride 5pc + Arreat's Bearing for triple Ancients. Rend — bleed DoT, apply and move on. Lunging Strike — best zero-unique starter, transitions to WW/HotA at 70.

Warlock

Soul Shards · Wrath + Dominance · Shadowform · Demonform · Sigils

Soul Shards

Mastermind (Laalish) — Abyss/stealth builds. Recasts don't break Shadowform, +30% Abyss in stealth. Ritualist (Vollach) — Occult/Overpower builds. Empowered Apocalypse. Vanguard — orbiting spell. Each has 2 Fragment upgrades.

Six distinct endgame builds. Blazing Abyss — permanent stealth, Abyss pulses. Apocalypse — 100-stack ramp → screen nuke, Hands of Worldbreaker makes it a Sigil skill. Dread Claws — best leveler, circular AoE from you AND demon. Abaddon Summoner — demon army, no uniques required. Blazing Scream — bouncing heads, Lesser Demon tag for unique synergies. Tyrant's Grasp — Demonform melee, Flesh of Abaddon talisman.

Paladin

Oaths · Arbiter Form · Auras · Faith · Resolve

Oaths & Arbiter Form

Disciple (most builds) — Disciple skill with CD → Arbiter form 4.5s. Arbiter = empowered Evade, damage scaling, Ascension buff. Judicator — Judgement detonation builds. Juggernaut — tank/thorns. Zealot — attack speed stacking. Resolve = defensive stacks, 20% DR while any remain.

Widest competitive spread of any class. Mobalytics has 10 S-tier Paladin builds. Auradin — kill by running near enemies, Holy Light Aura scales with Attack Speed + Armor (Castle paragon node), Dawnfire + Sundered Knight + Griswald (Cube). Hammerdin — D2 classic, Disciple's Halo orbits hammers, Argent Veil auto-casts via Arbiter Evade, needs boots with "Attacks Reduce Evade CD". Also S-tier: Wing Strike, Thorns/Shield of Retribution, Fist of Heavens, Zeal, Brandish, Blessed Shield, Support.

Rogue

Combo Points · Energy · Imbuements · Specializations

Specializations

Unlock lvl 15. Combo Points — build 3 with Basic, consume for damage + Lucky Hit. Nilfur's Narrow Eye 5pc auto-triggers Heartseeker 3× per Core cast = no manual Combo Points needed. Inner Sight — 25%× Crit Damage proc.

Death Trap (S+) — highest burst in game, trap + nuke. Dance of Knives (S) — channel indefinitely, snapshot Combo Points, Fan of Knives shotgun. Rapid Fire (A) — 10 arrows + Ruckus ricochet, cleanest leveling→endgame pipeline. Penetrating Shot (A) — Confluence groups enemies, pierce all. Barrage (A) — Windforce unique. Flurry (A) — melee hybrid, Transporter dash. All use Cold Imbuement → Freeze → Shatter. Swap Overflow → Shadow Dancer once energy is solved.

Sorcerer

Enchantments · Mana · Elemental Conversion · Unstable Currents · Crackling Energy

Enchantment System + LoH Changes

Slot skills for passive effects. LoH adds elemental conversion variants to every skill. Shock skill ranks are the meta stat — they scale damage, Unstable Currents duration, AND Crackling Energy generation simultaneously.

Ball Lightning / Ballkuna (S+) — consensus #1 overall build across all sources. Orbs travel slowly hitting everything repeatedly. Unstable Currents for screen-wide Crackling explosions. Static Field Blizzard / Shockuna (S) — Blizzard converted to Lightning variant, scales triple with Shock ranks. Fractured Winterglass chase unique. Hydra (A) — summon-caster turrets, fire/frost/lightning variants, great safety. Crackling Energy (A) — pure shock, low gear dependency. Fireball/Frozen Orb (B) — viable but lower ceiling than shock.

Druid

Spirit Boons · Shapeshifting · Companions · Storm · Form Selector

LoH Changes

New form selector node — pick Bear (tank) or Wolf (speed). Insatiable Fury cooldown removal for constant mobility. Spirit Boons grant passive bonuses.

Pulverize (S — Mobalytics) — Werebear slam, Overpower ground pound, simple and tanky. Rabies Lacerate (S — Icy Veins) — poison DoT werewolf, infect and spread plague. Wolf Companion (A) — pack leader pet build. Cataclysm (A) — storm ultimate. Stormslide (A) — hybrid storm+shape. Tornado (A–B) — classic storm. Shred (B) — werewolf melee. Boulder (B) — wrecking ball.

Game8 dropped Druid to B-tier overall due to high Pit struggles vs other classes. Still viable for all non-leaderboard content.

Necromancer

Book of the Dead · Essence · Minions · Corpse Skills · Direct Command

LoH Rework

Largest power jump of any returning class. Direct minion control — point warriors/mages at targets. Book of the Dead keeps minions alive during sacrifice. Sever gets built-in teleport via Inexorable Reaper. Skeletal Mages → core slot. Undercrown pushes warrior cap past 20.

Shadowblight (A) — no-minion Shadow DoT. Stack Blight, Corpse Explosion, Ebonpiercer, Soulrift. Trigger Shadowblight passive ASAP. Attack Speed is king. Blood Wave (A) — Kessime's Legacy pants, massive AoE via Blood rework. Summoner/Zerg (A) — "Lazy Necro Zerg" with 20+ warriors via Undercrown. Direct command for elite focus. Mendeln explosions chain. Bone Spirit (B) — burst single-target. Blood Lance (B) — ranged tether. Army of Dead (B) — ultimate burst. Sever (B) — fast blaster with teleport.

Spiritborn

Spirit Hall · Vigor · Guardian Spirits · Evade Mechanics

Spirit Hall

Primary + Secondary Guardian. Gorilla — all skills = Gorilla, Thorn damage, Barrier on attack. Eagle — evade resets, movement. Jaguar — max Ferocity, kill speed. Centipede — poison, sustain.

Under-the-radar this expansion. Evade Eagle (A) — evade spam with constant resets, Poison variant pushes high Pit. Quill Volley (A) — Rod of Kepeleke makes it Basic+Core. Gorilla primary + Jaguar secondary. Speed-farming king. Payback (A) — counter-based Gorilla tank. Rock Splitter (Leveling) — best Spiritborn leveler per Mobalytics.

Thunder Spike evade reset mechanic may have been relocated. Watch hotfix notes.

Post Level 70

Endgame Progression

  1. Hit 70 → Pit Tier 10 → Torment 1
  2. Respec skill tree for endgame variant
  3. Paragon — Legendary nodes → Glyphs to 15 (radius) → Glyphs to 46 (secondary)
  4. Talisman — Legendary Seal (+1 Charm Slot) → class BiS charm set (5pc) + 1 Unique Charm
  5. Horadric Cube — upgrade commons → legendary for target-farming
  6. Runewords — Ritual + Invocation in weapons/armor
  7. Masterworking — weapon damage → key offensive affix → capstone
  8. Torment push — T1→T2→T3→T4. Ancestral gear = guaranteed Greater Affix
  9. War Plans — Helltides, Pit, Undercity, Infernal Hordes, Echoing Hatred
  10. Boss Ladder — Lair → Greater Lair → Tormented (Mythic Unique target farm)
